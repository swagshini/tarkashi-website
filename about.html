About Tarkashi

The Art of Metal Inlay

Tarkashi is a magnificent traditional Indian craft that involves the delicate art of inlaying fine metal wires into wood to create intricate and mesmerizing designs. This ancient craft has been passed down through generations of skilled artisans.

Our Heritage

Each piece of Tarkashi work represents hours of meticulous craftsmanship, where artisans carefully embed brass or copper wires into precisely carved grooves in wooden surfaces. The result is a stunning fusion of metal and wood that creates beautiful geometric patterns and nature-inspired motifs.

The Process

The creation of Tarkashi involves multiple steps, from selecting the finest wood to carefully inlaying metal wires and finishing the piece to perfection. Our artisans combine traditional techniques with modern precision to create pieces that are both functional and artistic.
